Parameter listing

NameAvailabilityDescription
depthget The depth parameter signals the amount of data for the payment details provided. depth=0 is the highest level that provides full detail.
idget The id of the payment details in the system. This is guaranteed to be unique only over a single account.
xidget The xid of the payment details in the system. This is guaranteed to be unique over all accounts.
clientget The client the payment was for.
typeget The type of payment details (usually bank_account or credit_card).
bankget The bank the payment details are with. Will also list visa/mastercard/etc in the case of credit cards.
numberget The credit card or bank account number. Hidden digits are replaced with *'s.
expirygetupdate The expiry date for credit cards (null on bank accounts)
last4get The last four unencrypted digits of the bank account number or credit card.
stateget The current state of the payment details (active, disabled, expired or dead).
